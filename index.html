How to Play

🎯 Game Objective

This game is about understanding what happens to different items when they end up in landfills. Your task is to sort items based on their environmental impact when discarded.

🌍 Understanding the Zones

Safe Zone
Items that decompose naturally and safely return to the environment without releasing harmful toxins
Harmful Zone
Items that release toxins or take extremely long to decompose, causing environmental harm

🎮 How to Play

  • Tap an item in the unsorted zone to select it
  • Tap a zone (Safe or Harmful) to move the selected item there
  • You have 60 seconds to sort all items correctly
  • Items in the Safe zone decompose naturally and safely
  • Items in the Harmful zone release toxins or take centuries to decompose
  • Get immediate feedback with facts about each item when sorted correctly

🏆 Scoring

  • Score 60% or higher to earn a ROLL of Earthya Compostable Bags
  • Score below 60% to earn a PACK of Earthya Compostable Bags
  • Learn from your mistakes with detailed explanations for incorrect placements
Time: 60s
